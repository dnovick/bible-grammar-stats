BBA Chapter 4 — Noun Identification Drill

Instructions: For each Aramaic noun form: (1) Gender — m. or f., (2) Number — s., pl., or du., (3) State — abs., det., or cstr., (4) Root / Lexical Form — the dictionary entry this form derives from. All forms are in the absolute state.

Reminder — Aramaic absolute-state endings: Masculine singular: no ending · Feminine singular: ָה- · Masculine plural: ִין- · Feminine plural: ָן-
