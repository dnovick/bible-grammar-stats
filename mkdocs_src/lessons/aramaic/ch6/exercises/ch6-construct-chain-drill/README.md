# "Construct Chain Drill" — BBA Ch6 Nouns: Construct State

## Description

20-item drill covering the construct-state paradigm introduced in Chapter 6. Each item presents the absolute form of the first noun (nomen regens) along with a genitive noun (nomen rectum); students write the construct form of the first noun and complete the chain. Items draw on vocabulary from Chapters 4, 5, and 6 and cover all four gender/number construct patterns. The drill includes masculine singular, feminine singular, masculine plural, and feminine plural construct forms, with special attention to the key feminine singular construct שַׁעַת (from שָׁעָה) and lamed-waw feminines (רְבוּ, שָׁלוּ).
