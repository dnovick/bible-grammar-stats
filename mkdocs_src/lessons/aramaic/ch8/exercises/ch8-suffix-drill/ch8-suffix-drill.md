# Ch8 Suffix Drill — BBA Pronominal Suffixes

*Basics of Biblical Aramaic, Van Pelt — Chapter 8*

---

## Instructions

Each item gives an Aramaic noun or prepositional phrase with a pronominal suffix, drawn from Daniel and Ezra. Your tasks:

1. Parse the **base form** (noun or preposition, with its lexical form).
2. Identify the **pronominal suffix** (person, gender, number).
3. Give the **English translation**.

## Reflection Questions

1. Items 1 and 14 both use the 1cs suffix on nouns. What is the suffix form? How does it compare to the Hebrew 1cs suffix on singular nouns?

2. Item 7 (מִנִּי) shows מִן with the 1cs suffix. Why is there a dagesh forte in the נ? What phonological rule explains this?

3. Items 5 and 13 both end in ֵהּ-. One is a noun (house) and one is a noun (God). Both carry the 3ms suffix. What vowel precedes the suffix in each case, and what does that tell you about the conditioning of the ֵהּ- form?

4. Items 8 and 19 both involve plural nouns with suffixes. What is the connecting element between the noun stem and the suffix? How does this compare to Hebrew masculine plural nouns with pronominal suffixes?
