## Answer Key

| # | Form | Tense | Voice | Mood | Person | Number | Lexical form | Translation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | λύω | Present | Active | Indicative | 1st | Singular | λύω | I am loosing / I loose |
| 2 | λύεις | Present | Active | Indicative | 2nd | Singular | λύω | You are loosing |
| 3 | λύει | Present | Active | Indicative | 3rd | Singular | λύω | He/she/it is loosing |
| 4 | λύομεν | Present | Active | Indicative | 1st | Plural | λύω | We are loosing |
| 5 | λύετε | Present | Active | Indicative | 2nd | Plural | λύω | You (pl.) are loosing |
| 6 | λύουσιν | Present | Active | Indicative | 3rd | Plural | λύω | They are loosing |
| 7 | πιστεύω | Present | Active | Indicative | 1st | Singular | πιστεύω | I believe / I am believing |

> **Parsing tip:** The -ουσι(ν) ending (3pl) is the most recognizable PAI ending in the GNT. The movable ν appears when the next word begins with a vowel or at the end of a sentence.
