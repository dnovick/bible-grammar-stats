# Exercise: Ch17 Contract Verb Parsing

**Chapter:** BBG Ch17 — Contract Verbs
**Type:** Morphological parsing drill with contraction analysis
**Items:** 20

## Description

Parse each contracted verb form (person, number, lexical form) and identify the two vowels that contracted to produce the form. All verbs are present active indicative. Forms are drawn from all three contract classes: α-contract, ε-contract, and ο-contract.

## Coverage

| Contract class | Representative verb | Forms drilled |
|---|---|---|
| α-contract (-αω) | ἀγαπάω | All 6 persons |
| ε-contract (-εω) | ποιέω, λαλέω, ζητέω | All 6 persons |
| ο-contract (-οω) | πληρόω | Selected forms |
