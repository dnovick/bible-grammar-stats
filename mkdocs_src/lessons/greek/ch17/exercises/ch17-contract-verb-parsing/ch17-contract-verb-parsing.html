BBG Chapter 17 — Contract Verb Parsing Drill

For each contracted form: (1) give person and number, (2) give the lexical form (-αω/-εω/-οω), (3) identify the two vowels that contracted (e.g. "ε + ο"), and (4) translate. All forms are present active indicative. Click ▶ Answer to check.
