BBH Chapter 33 · Pual Weak Verbs

Each triplet shows the same weak root in Qal, Piel, and Pual. For each form: (1) identify the stem, (2) parse fully, (3) translate.

StemR1 vowel (Perfect 3ms)R2 / EndFunction
QalVariable (qamets, etc.)Normal for weak typeSimple active

III-ה Pual perfect 3ms: ends in ָּה (qamets-heh) — the ה quiesces and the qibbuts + dagesh pattern appears in R1–R2.
I-Guttural / R2-guttural: gutturals reject dagesh forte — look for compensatory lengthening (qibbuts → holem, hiriq → tsere).
