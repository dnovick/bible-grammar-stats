Quick test: Does R1 have qibbuts (ֻ) or holem where a guttural R2 blocks dagesh? → Pual. Does R1 have hiriq/tsere with dagesh forte in R2? → Piel. Plain vowel, no dagesh forte in R2? → Qal.
