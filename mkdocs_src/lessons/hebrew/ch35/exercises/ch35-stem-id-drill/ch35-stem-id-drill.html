Watch: Piel הִלֵּל begins with הִ– — looks like Hiphil. But there is no ת after הִ, and the Dagesh is in R2 (לּ) with Tsere → Piel, not Hiphil or Hithpael.

22 מְגַלֶּה

23 גֹּלֶה

24 מִתְגַּלֶּה
