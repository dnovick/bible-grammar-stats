# Ch5 Exercise — Definite Article and Conjunction ו

Identify the definite article and conjunction ו in Hebrew words and phrases, determine the specific form of each, and translate.

| Part | Description | Items |
|---|---|---|

| Part B | Article before gutturals — הֶ (segol) and הָ (qamets) forms | 5 |
| Part C | Conjunction ו only — וְ, וּ, and וָ forms | 5 |
| Part D | Both article and conjunction present | 5 |
| Part E | Neither article nor conjunction (contrast items) | 2 |
