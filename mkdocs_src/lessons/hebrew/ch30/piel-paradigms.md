# Piel Paradigm Tables — דִּבֵּר

*Piel paradigm root: דִּבֵּר (to speak) — strong triliteral, denominative*
*With Qal reference column for comparison*

---

## Perfect (Qatal)

| Person | Qal (ref.) | **Piel** (דבר) |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | קָטַל | **דִּבֵּר** |
| 3fs | קָטְלָה | **דִּבְּרָה** |
| 2ms | קָטַלְתָּ | **דִּבַּרְתָּ** |
| 2fs | קָטַלְתְּ | **דִּבַּרְתְּ** |
| 1cs | קָטַלְתִּי | **דִּבַּרְתִּי** |
| 3cp | קָטְלוּ | **דִּבְּרוּ** |
| 2mp | קְטַלְתֶּם | **דִּבַּרְתֶּם** |
| 2fp | קְטַלְתֶּן | **דִּבַּרְתֶּן** |
| 1cp | קָטַלְנוּ | **דִּבַּרְנוּ** |

> **Key marker:** Hireq (ִ) under R1 + **Dagesh Forte in R2** + Tsere (ֵ) under R2 in 3ms/3fs. The dagesh in R2 is the single most reliable Piel identifier across all conjugations.

| Person | **Piel** |
|---|---|
| 3ms | **וַיְדַבֵּר** |
| 3fs | **וַתְּדַבֵּר** |
| 3mp | **וַיְדַבְּרוּ** |
| 1cs | **וָאֲדַבֵּר** |
| 1cp | **וַנְּדַבֵּר** |

> **Key marker:** וַיְ prefix (patach + shewa). Contrast Qal וַיִּ and Hiphil וַיַּ — the shewa under the prefix consonant is diagnostic of the Piel.

## Imperative

| Form | **Piel** |
|---|---|
| 2ms | **דַּבֵּר** |
| 2fs | **דַּבְּרִי** |
| 2mp | **דַּבְּרוּ** |
| 2fp | **דַּבֵּרְנָה** |

> **Key marker:** Patach (ַ) under R1 + Dagesh Forte in R2 + Tsere under R2 (2ms). Form is identical to the Infinitive Construct — context and prefixed prepositions distinguish them.

---

## Infinitive Construct

| Form | **Piel** | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Base | **דַּבֵּר** | identical to Imperative 2ms |
| + לְ | **לְדַבֵּר** | |
| + בְּ | **בְּדַבֵּר** | temporal: "when speaking" |

> **Key marker:** Patach under R1 + dagesh in R2 + Tsere. Prepositions (לְ, בְּ, כְּ) almost always signal the Inf. Construct rather than the Imperative.

---

## Infinitive Absolute

| Form | **Piel** |
|---|---|
| Standard | **דַּבֵּר** |

> **Key marker:** Same form as Imperative/Inf. Construct. Used in cognate constructions: דַּבֵּר יְדַבֵּר "he will certainly speak."

---

## Participle (Active)

| Form | **Piel** |
|---|---|
| ms | **מְדַבֵּר** |
| fs | **מְדַבֶּרֶת** |
| mp | **מְדַבְּרִים** |
| fp | **מְדַבְּרוֹת** |

> **Key marker:** מְ prefix + Patach under R1 + dagesh in R2 + Tsere under R2 (ms). The מְ prefix is shared with the Hiphil participle — the dagesh in R2 distinguishes Piel from Hiphil (no dagesh in Hiphil R2).

---

## Summary: Qal vs. Piel at a Glance

| Conjugation | Qal marker | **Piel marker** |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect 3ms | קָטַל | **דִּ + dagesh + Tsere** |
| Imperfect 3ms | יִקְטֹל (no dagesh in R2) | **יְ + Patach + dagesh + Tsere** |
| Wayyiqtol | וַיִּ prefix | **וַיְ prefix** |
| Weqatal | וְקָ | **וְדִ** |
| Imperative 2ms | no prefix | **דַּ + dagesh + Tsere** |
| Inf. Construct | קְטֹל | **דַּבֵּר** |
| Inf. Absolute | קָטוֹל | **דַּבֵּר** |
| Participle ms | קֹטֵל | **מְדַבֵּר** |

**The single most reliable Piel identifier: Dagesh Forte in R2 (the middle root consonant).**
