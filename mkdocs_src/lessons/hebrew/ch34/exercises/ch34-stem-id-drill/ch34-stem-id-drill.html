Key distinctions: Piel vs. Hithpael: both have Dagesh in R2 — the הִתְ prefix marks Hithpael. Hiphil vs. Hithpael: Hiphil has יַ– prefix (imperfect) and מַ– (participle); Hithpael has יִתְ– and מִתְ–.
Note on items 3 and 20: הִתְקַדֵּשׁ is identical for Hithpael Perfect 3ms and Hithpael Imperative 2ms. Both answers are correct; context in a sentence disambiguates.

3 הִתְקַדֵּשׁ
Hithpael · Perfect 3ms OR Imperative 2ms · קדשׁ — הִתְ prefix + Dagesh in R2 → Hithpael; both parsings are identical in writing for strong roots
4 נִקְדַּשׁ
Niphal · Perfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — נִ– prefix + Dagesh in R1 (assimilation of nun) → Niphal perfect
5 הִקְדִּישׁ
Hiphil · Perfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — הִ– prefix + Hireq-Yod after R2 (–ִישׁ) → Hiphil perfect strong pattern
6 קֻדַּשׁ
Pual · Perfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — Qibbuts under R1 + Dagesh in R2 → Pual (passive of Piel)
7 דִּבֶּר
Piel · Perfect · 3ms · דבר — Hireq under R1, Dagesh in R2 (בּ), Seghol under R2 → Piel perfect 3ms
8 דָּבַר

9 הִתְדַּבֵּר
Hithpael · Perfect · 3ms · דבר — הִתְ– prefix + Dagesh in R2 (בּ) → Hithpael; root דבר has no sibilant, so no metathesis
10 יְקַדֵּשׁ
Piel · Imperfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — יְ– prefix (shewa), Patakh under R1, Dagesh in R2 → Piel imperfect
11 יִקְדַּשׁ
Qal · Imperfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — יִ– prefix (Hireq), Shewa under R1, Patakh under R2, no Dagesh in R2 → Qal
12 יִתְקַדֵּשׁ
Hithpael · Imperfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — יִתְ– prefix + Dagesh in R2 → Hithpael imperfect (יִ– + תְ replaces the הִ of the perfect)
13 יִקָּדֵשׁ
Niphal · Imperfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — יִקָּ–: nun assimilates into R1 with Dagesh + Qamets → Niphal imperfect strong pattern
14 יַקְדִּישׁ
Hiphil · Imperfect · 3ms · קדשׁ — יַ– prefix (Patakh), Hireq-Yod after R2 (–ִישׁ) → Hiphil imperfect
15 יְקֻדַּשׁ

16 וַיְקַדֵּשׁ

17 וַיִּקְדַּשׁ
Qal · Wayyiqtol · 3ms · קדשׁ — וַ + יִ– (Hireq + Dagesh Forte in yod) + no Dagesh in R2 → Qal wayyiqtol
18 וַיִּתְקַדֵּשׁ
Hithpael · Wayyiqtol · 3ms · קדשׁ — וַ + יִתְ– + Dagesh in R2 → Hithpael wayyiqtol
19 קַדֵּשׁ
Piel · Imperative · 2ms · קדשׁ — Patakh under R1 + Dagesh in R2 + Tsere under R2 → Piel imperative 2ms (= Piel imperfect with prefix dropped)
20 הִתְקַדֵּשׁ
Hithpael · Imperative · 2ms · קדשׁ — הִתְ– prefix + Dagesh in R2 → Hithpael imperative; form is identical to Hithpael Perfect 3ms (item 3) for strong roots
21 הִקָּדֵשׁ
Niphal · Imperative · 2ms · קדשׁ — הִ– prefix + Dagesh in R1 (nun assimilated) + Qamets → Niphal imperative strong
22 מְקַדֵּשׁ
Piel · Participle · ms · קדשׁ — מְ– prefix (Shewa) + Patakh R1 + Dagesh R2 + Tsere → Piel active participle
23 מִתְקַדֵּשׁ
Hithpael · Participle · ms · קדשׁ — מִתְ– prefix (Hireq + ת) + Dagesh R2 → Hithpael participle (מִ replaces הִ of perfect; תְ is retained)
24 מַקְדִּישׁ
Hiphil · Participle · ms · קדשׁ — מַ– prefix (Patakh) + Hireq-Yod after R2 (–ִישׁ) → Hiphil participle
