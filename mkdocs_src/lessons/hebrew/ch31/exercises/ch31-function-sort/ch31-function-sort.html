BBH Chapter 31 · Piel Weak Verbs

Classify each Piel verb as I (Intensive), F (Factitive), D (Declarative), SA (Simple Action), or DN (Denominative). All roots are weak. Type your answer in the Function column, then click ▶ Answer to check.

CodeFunctionKey diagnostic question
IIntensiveDoes the subject do the Qal action more forcefully, completely, or repeatedly?
FFactitiveDoes the subject cause an object to enter a state? (Stative Qal root.)
DDeclarativeDoes the subject pronounce or treat something as being in a state?
SASimple ActionIs the Piel the standard form with no common Qal equivalent?
DNDenominativeIs the Piel verb built on a noun and performs that noun's characteristic action?
Tip: Stative root (to be X) + Piel = Piel causes X → Factitive. Active root (to do X) + Piel = Piel does X more intensely → Intensive. No common Qal → Simple Action. Built on a noun → Denominative.

1 בֵּרַךְ Perfect 3ms ברך Gen 1:28 "God blessed them"
I — Intensive · Root: ברך (Qal: to kneel/bless — rare) · Piel = formal, intensive bestowal of divine favor; the standard OT word for blessing
2 גִּלָּה Perfect 3ms גלה Gen 35:7 "God revealed himself to him"
I — Intensive · Root: גלה (Qal: to uncover / go into exile) · Piel = reveal completely, disclose thoroughly. III-ה root; dagesh in ל
3 חִזֵּק Perfect 3ms חזק Exo 4:21 "I will harden his heart"
F — Factitive · Root: חזק (Qal: to be strong/hard — stative) · Piel = cause to enter the state of hardness; "he hardened [Pharaoh's heart]." Classic Factitive
4 כִּלָּה Perfect 3ms כלה Gen 2:2 "God finished his work"
I or F — Intensive / Factitive · Root: כלה (Qal: to be complete/finished — stative) · Factitive: cause to reach completion; Intensive: finish thoroughly. Either analysis is acceptable
5 צִוָּה Perfect 3ms צוה Gen 2:16 "the LORD commanded the man"
SA — Simple Action · Root: צוה (no common Qal) · Commanding is the Piel's own basic function. III-ה root
6 בֵּרֲכוּ Perfect 3cp ברך — "they blessed"
I — Intensive · Root: ברך (same as #1) · Note hateph-patach under ר in 3cp form — I-guttural effect in conjugation. Function identical to #1
7 יְבָרֵךְ Imperfect 3ms ברך Psa 115:12 "the LORD will bless"
I — Intensive · Root: ברך · Piel imperfect 3ms; shewa under יְ + qamets + tsere = Piel imperfect marker. Blessing = intensive/formal divine action
8 יְצַוֶּה Imperfect 3ms צוה — "he will command"
SA — Simple Action · Root: צוה (no common Qal) · Piel imperfect 3ms; note III-ה contraction with tsere-yod pattern. Function identical to #5
9 וַיְבָרֶךְ Wayyiqtol 3ms ברך Gen 1:22 "God blessed them"
I — Intensive · Root: ברך · Waw-consecutive + Piel imperfect; same function as #1 and #7
10 וַיְצַו Wayyiqtol 3ms צוה Gen 3:11 "which I commanded you"
SA — Simple Action · Root: צוה · Wayyiqtol 3ms; III-ה root contracts (ה drops in wayyiqtol). No Qal; commanding = Piel primary
11 חִיָּה Perfect 3ms חיה Gen 47:25 "you have kept us alive"
F — Factitive · Root: חיה (Qal: to live/be alive — stative) · Piel = cause to be alive = preserve, keep alive. Classic Factitive
12 שִׁלֵּם Perfect 3ms שׁלם Exo 22:2 "he shall make full restitution"
I or F — Intensive / Factitive · Root: שׁלם (Qal: to be complete/at peace — stative) · Factitive: cause completion (pay in full); Intensive: pay thoroughly. Legal "make restitution" = both analyses are valid
13 עִנָּה Perfect 3ms ענה Gen 15:13 "they will afflict them"
F — Factitive · Root: ענה II (Qal: to be afflicted/humbled — stative; distinct from ענה I "answer") · Piel = cause to be in a state of affliction. I-guttural (ע) + III-ה
14 כִּנָּה Perfect 3ms כנה Isa 45:4 "I gave you a title"
DN — Denominative · Root: כנה (noun: כִּנּוּי "title/surname/honorific") · To give someone a title or name. God assigns an honorific to Cyrus. Denominative = verb performs the noun's characteristic action
15 בִּקֵּשׁ Perfect 3ms בקש Gen 37:15 "what are you seeking?"
SA — Simple Action · Root: בקש (no common Qal) · Seeking/searching is the Piel's primary function; strong root but no Qal counterpart in standard use
16 יְבַקֵּשׁ Imperfect 3ms בקש — "he will seek"
SA — Simple Action · Root: בקש · Piel imperfect 3ms; same analysis as #15
17 גִּלָּה Perfect 3ms גלה Amo 3:7 "he reveals his secret to his servants"
I — Intensive · Root: גלה (same form as #2) · Different context: God discloses prophetic secrets to his prophets. Thorough, intentional revelation. Same analysis as #2
18 שִׁנָּה Perfect 3ms שׁנה — "he changed / altered"
I — Intensive · Root: שׁנה (Qal: to change/repeat) · Piel = alter thoroughly, change completely. III-ה root; dagesh in נ
19 חִזְּקוּ Perfect 3cp חזק — "they strengthened / encouraged"
F — Factitive · Root: חזק (Qal: to be strong — stative) · Piel 3cp = they caused [others] to be strong = they strengthened/encouraged. Same analysis as #3
20 בִּנָּה Perfect 3ms בנה 1 Kgs 6:2 "Solomon built the house"
I — Intensive · Root: בנה (Qal: to build) · Piel = build thoroughly, construct comprehensively. Used of Solomon's comprehensive temple-building program. III-ה root; dagesh in נ
