# Exercise: Future Active/Middle Parsing Drill

**Chapter:** BBG Ch19 — Future Active and Middle Indicative
**Type:** Morphology parsing drill
**Items:** 20

## Description

Twenty future active and middle indicative forms drawn from common GNT verbs. Students parse each form (tense, voice, mood, person, number) and provide the lexical form and a translation. Forms include regular futures, stop-consonant futures (ψ/ξ/σ combinations), liquid futures, and contract verb futures.

## Conjugation Coverage

| Form Type | Items |
|---|---|
| Regular future active | 6 |
| Stop-consonant future active (labial/velar/dental) | 5 |
| Liquid future active | 3 |
| Contract verb future active | 3 |
| Future middle (regular) | 3 |
