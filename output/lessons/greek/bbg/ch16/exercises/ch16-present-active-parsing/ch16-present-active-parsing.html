BBG Chapter 16 — Present Active Indicative Parsing Drill

Parse each verb: Tense · Voice · Mood · Person · Number · Lexical form · Translation. All forms are present active indicative. Click ▶ Answer to check your work.

# Form Person Number Lexical form Translation
1 λύω
Pres. Act. Ind. · 1st · Singular · λύω — "I am loosing / I loose"
2 λύεις
