Chapter 9 — Adjective Parsing Drill

BBG Chapter 9 · Adjectives

For each phrase, parse the adjective: Case (N/G/D/A), Number (S/P), Gender (M/F/N), and Position (Attr / Pred / Sub).

Key rule: If the article precedes the adjective directly → attributive or substantival. If the noun has an article but the adjective does not → predicate. If article + adjective but no noun → substantival.
