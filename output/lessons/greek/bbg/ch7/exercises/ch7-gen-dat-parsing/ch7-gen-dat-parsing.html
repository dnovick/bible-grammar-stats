BBG Chapter 7 · 2nd-Declension Genitive and Dative Forms

Parse each Greek form: Case (G/D), Number (S/P), Gender (M/N), Lexical Form, and a brief Translation Note.

Reminder: The dative singular ends in -ῳ (omega with iota subscript); the genitive singular ends in -ου. The article is your best guide: τοῦ = genitive singular; τῷ = dative singular; τῶν = genitive plural; τοῖς = dative plural.
