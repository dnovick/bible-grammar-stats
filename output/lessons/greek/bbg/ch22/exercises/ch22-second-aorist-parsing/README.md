# Exercise: Second Aorist Parsing Drill

**Chapter:** BBG Ch22 — Second Aorist Active and Middle Indicative
**Type:** Morphology parsing drill
**Items:** 20

## Description

Twenty second aorist active and middle indicative forms from the GNT's most frequent verbs. Students parse each form (tense, voice, mood, person, number), identify the lexical form (present), and distinguish from imperfect forms using the stem-comparison method.

## Coverage

| Form Type | Items |
|---|---|
| 2nd aorist active — from major suppletive verbs | 10 |
| 2nd aorist active — other verbs | 4 |
| 2nd aorist middle | 4 |
| Imperfect forms included as distractors | 2 |
