# Exercise: Aorist and Future Passive Parsing Drill

**Chapter:** BBG Ch24 — Aorist and Future Passive Indicative
**Type:** Morphology parsing drill
**Items:** 20

## Description

Twenty aorist passive and future passive indicative forms drawn from GNT vocabulary. Students parse each form (tense, voice, mood, person, number), identify the lexical form, and translate. Selected items practice recognizing the θη formant, the future passive θησ+middle endings pattern, and deponent passives.

## Coverage

| Form Type | Items |
|---|---|
| Aorist passive — regular (θη formant) | 8 |
| Aorist passive — η variant | 3 |
| Aorist passive — deponent | 2 |
| Future passive | 5 |
| Active/middle distractors | 2 |
