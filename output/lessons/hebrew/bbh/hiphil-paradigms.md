# Hiphil (הִפְעִיל) — Full Conjugation Paradigms

*Model root: קטל (Q = קָטַל). All forms are strong-root paradigms.*
*Source: Basics of Biblical Hebrew, Pratico & Van Pelt, Ch. 26*

---

## Perfect (Qatal) {#perfect}

*BBH §26.3 — Prefix: הִ; characteristic vowel: chiriq under first root letter*

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | הִקְטִיל | — |
| 3fs | הִקְטִילָה | הִקְטִילוּ |
| 2ms | הִקְטַלְתָּ | הִקְטַלְתֶּם |
| 2fs | הִקְטַלְתְּ | הִקְטַלְתֶּן |
| 1cs | הִקְטַלְתִּי | הִקְטַלְנוּ |

> **Key marker:** הִ prefix + *chiriq* vowel pattern. Distinguished from Niphal perfect (נִקְטַל) by the *chiriq* in the second syllable vs. Niphal's *patach*.

---

## Imperfect (Yiqtol) {#imperfect}

*BBH §26.5 — Prefix vowel: patach (יַ); characteristic: chiriq in final syllable*

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יַקְטִיל | יַקְטִילוּ |
| 3fs | תַּקְטִיל | תַּקְטֵלְנָה |
| 2ms | תַּקְטִיל | תַּקְטִילוּ |
| 2fs | תַּקְטִילִי | תַּקְטֵלְנָה |
| 1cs | אַקְטִיל | נַקְטִיל |

> **Key marker:** *Patach* under prefix consonant (יַ/תַּ/אַ/נַ) + *chiriq* in second syllable. Distinguishes from Qal (יִקְטֹל — *hiriq* + *holem*) and Piel (יְקַטֵּל — *shewa* + *patach-dagesh*).

## Wayyiqtol {#wayyiqtol}

*Sequential narrative form of the imperfect. Prefix: וַיַּ; final syllable shifts from chiriq to tsere.*

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | וַיַּקְטֵל | וַיַּקְטִילוּ |
| 3fs | וַתַּקְטֵל | וַתַּקְטֵלְנָה |
| 2ms | וַתַּקְטֵל | וַתַּקְטִילוּ |
| 1cs | וָאַקְטֵל | וַנַּקְטֵל |

> **Key marker:** Doubled prefix consonant (יַּ/תַּ) + *tsere* in final syllable. The vowel shift (chiriq → tsere) from the yiqtol is the diagnostic difference within the Hiphil itself.

---

## Weqatal {#weqatal}

*Sequential future form of the perfect. וְ + perfect form; used for sequential or consequential future.*

| Person | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | וְהִקְטִיל | וְהִקְטִילוּ |
| 3fs | וְהִקְטִילָה | — |
| 2ms | וְהִקְטַלְתָּ | וְהִקְטַלְתֶּם |
| 1cs | וְהִקְטַלְתִּי | וְהִקְטַלְנוּ |

> **Key marker:** וְ + identical to the perfect paradigm. Context (future/sequential meaning) distinguishes it from a simple perfect with vav-conjunction.

## Imperative {#imperative}

*BBH §26.7 — Prefix: הַ; tsere in final syllable*

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| 2ms | הַקְטֵל | הַקְטִילוּ |
| 2fs | הַקְטִילִי | הַקְטֵלְנָה |

> **Key marker:** הַ prefix + *tsere* final (2ms) or *chiriq* (2fs/2mp). Note that the imperative and infinitive absolute share the same 2ms base form.

---

## Infinitive Construct {#infinitive-construct}

*BBH §26.9 — Prefix: הַ; chiriq in final syllable*

| Form | Notes |
|---|---|
| הַקְטִיל | Base form |
| לְהַקְטִיל | With לְ preposition (purpose/complement) |
| בְּהַקְטִיל | With בְּ (temporal: "when/while causing") |
| כְּהַקְטִיל | With כְּ (temporal: "when/as causing") |

> **Key marker:** הַ prefix + *chiriq* final. Identical in form to the perfect 3ms (הִקְטִיל) except for the vowel under הַ (*patach* vs. *hiriq*). Context and prepositions clarify function.

---

## Infinitive Absolute {#infinitive-absolute}

*BBH §26.11 — Prefix: הַ; tsere in final syllable*

| Form | Notes |
|---|---|
| הַקְטֵל | Standard form |

> **Usage:** Appears before or after a finite verb of the same root for emphasis (הַרְבָּה אַרְבֶּה "I will surely multiply," Gen 3:16). Less common in the Hiphil than in the Qal.

---

## Participle (Active) {#participle}

*BBH §26.13 — Prefix: מַ; chiriq in final syllable*

| | Singular | Plural |
|---|---|---|
| ms | מַקְטִיל | מַקְטִילִים |
| fs | מַקְטֶלֶת | מַקְטִילוֹת |

> **Key marker:** מַ prefix + *chiriq* (ms) or *tsere-seghol* (fs). Identical prefix to the Piel participle (מְקַטֵּל) but with different internal vowels — *patach* under מַ vs. *shewa* under מְ.
