Identify the person, number, gender, and root (3ms lexical form) for each of 25 Qal Perfect forms.

| Part | Description | Items |
|---|---|---|

| Part B | Vowel reduction in R1 (3cp, 2mp, 2fp) and accent variation | 10 |
| Part C | Stative roots — identify type (A/B/C) and give the 3ms dictionary form | 5 |
