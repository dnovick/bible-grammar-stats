# Ch13 Passage Exercise — Qal Perfect Strong Verbs

*Parse each Qal Perfect verb. Give: (a) Person, (b) Number, (c) Gender, (d) Root (3ms lexical form), (e) Usage Type (Simple Past / Perfect of Experience / Stative / Prophetic Perfect).*

*Note: Several of the highest-frequency Torah verbs are weak roots (III-ה, I-נ, etc.). For this exercise, focus on the suffix pattern and the usage type; weak-root vowel changes are covered in Chapter 14.*
