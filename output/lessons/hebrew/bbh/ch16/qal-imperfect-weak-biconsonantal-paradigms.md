> **Key markers:**
> - Long Imperfect: qamets under prefix + shureq as middle vowel (יָקוּם)
> - Short Jussive: qamets hatuf (ָׇ) in the root — a very short vowel
> - Wayyiqtol: same as Jussive + waw-consecutive: וַיָּ֫קׇם (qamets under the doubled prefix consonant)
