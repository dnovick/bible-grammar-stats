| 3fp | תִּבְנֶ֫ינָה | — | — | Rare; seghol-he + נָה |
| 2mp | תִּבְנ֫וּ | תִּבְנ֫וּ | — | Same as 3mp |

| 1cp | נִבְנֶ֫ה | נִ֫בֶן | — | |

> **Key markers:**
> - Long Imperfect: ends in **-ֶה** (seghol + he)
> - Short Jussive: final ה drops; preceding consonant takes seghol or patach
> - Wayyiqtol: waw-consecutive prefix + short (jussive) form
> - 3mp/2mp: -וּ replaces the ה (same in both long and short)

| 3fs | תִּהְיֶ֫ה | תְּהִ֫י | — | |

| 1cp | נִהְיֶ֫ה | — | — | |

> **Note on הָיָה:** The jussive 3ms is יְהִי (hireq-yod), not the expected seghol form.
> This is one of the most distinctive and frequent forms in the entire OT — "Let there be..."
> (Gen 1:3). It must be memorized.
