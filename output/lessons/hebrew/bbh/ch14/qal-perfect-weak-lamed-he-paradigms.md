# Qal Perfect — III-ה (Lamed-He) Paradigms

*Full inflection tables for III-ה weak roots in the Qal Perfect.*

---

## III-ה: עָשָׂה (do, make)

| Person | Form | Suffix | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3ms | עָשָׂה | ָה- | Qamets + mappiq-less he (vowel letter) |
| 3fs | עָשְׂתָה | ְתָה- | Shewa under R1; -תָה suffix (not just ָה-) |
| 2ms | עָשִׂיתָ | ִיתָ- | Hireq-yod before suffix — key diagnostic |
| 2fs | עָשִׂית | ִית- | Same root; shorter suffix |
| 1cs | עָשִׂיתִי | ִיתִי- | Hireq-yod throughout |
| 3cp | עָשׂוּ | וּ- | He drops entirely before -וּ |
| 2mp | עֲשִׂיתֶם | ִיתֶם- | R1 reduces (chateph under ע) |
| 2fp | עֲשִׂיתֶן | ִיתֶן- | Same as 2mp; nun final |
| 1cp | עָשִׂינוּ | ִינוּ- | Hireq-yod + נוּ |

> **Key:** The hireq-yod suffix (ִי- before consonantal endings) distinguishes III-ה in the
> Perfect from all other weak classes. The 3ms ends in ָה-, but the 3fs ends in ְתָה- (not just
> ָה- like the strong 3fs). The he drops in the 3cp before -וּ.

## III-ה: הָיָה (be, become)

| Person | Form | Suffix | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3ms | הָיָה | ָה- | R1 ה (guttural) |
| 3fs | הָיְתָה | ְתָה- | Shewa under R1; -תָה ending |
| 2ms | הָיִיתָ | ִיתָ- | Doubled yod visible in some traditions |
| 2fs | הָיִית | ִית- | |
| 1cs | הָיִיתִי | ִיתִי- | |
| 3cp | הָיוּ | וּ- | He drops |
| 2mp | הֱיִיתֶם | — | R1 takes chateph-seghol (guttural); irregular |
| 1cp | הָיִינוּ | ִינוּ- | |

> **Note on הָיָה:** The 3cp form הָיוּ is extremely common in narrative. The 3ms הָיָה
> (with accent on final syllable) is nearly identical in appearance to the Qal Perfect 3fs of
> regular roots ending in ָה-; context determines parsing.
