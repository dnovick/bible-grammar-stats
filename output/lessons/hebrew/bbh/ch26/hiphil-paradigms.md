# Hiphil (הִפְעִיל) — Full Conjugation Paradigms

*Model root: קטל (Qal: קָטַל). All forms are strong-root paradigms.*
*Qal column is included for direct comparison at each conjugation.*
*Source: Basics of Biblical Hebrew, Pratico & Van Pelt, Ch. 26*

---

## Perfect (Qatal)

*BBH §26.3 — Hiphil prefix: הִ; characteristic vowel: chiriq under first root letter*

| Person | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | קָטַל | **הִקְטִיל** |
| 3fs | קָטְלָה | **הִקְטִילָה** |
| 3cp | קָטְלוּ | **הִקְטִילוּ** |
| 2ms | קָטַלְתָּ | **הִקְטַלְתָּ** |
| 2mp | קְטַלְתֶּם | **הִקְטַלְתֶּם** |
| 2fs | קָטַלְתְּ | **הִקְטַלְתְּ** |
| 2fp | קְטַלְתֶּן | **הִקְטַלְתֶּן** |
| 1cs | קָטַלְתִּי | **הִקְטַלְתִּי** |
| 1cp | קָטַלְנוּ | **הִקְטַלְנוּ** |

> **Key marker:** הִ prefix + *chiriq* vowel pattern distinguishes Hiphil from Qal (*patach-patach*) and Niphal (נִקְטַל, *hiriq-patach*).

## Imperfect (Yiqtol)

*BBH §26.5 — Hiphil prefix vowel: patach (יַ); characteristic: chiriq in final syllable*

| Person | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יִקְטֹל | **יַקְטִיל** |
| 3fs | תִּקְטֹל | **תַּקְטִיל** |
| 3mp | יִקְטְלוּ | **יַקְטִילוּ** |
| 3fp | תִּקְטֹלְנָה | **תַּקְטֵלְנָה** |
| 2ms | תִּקְטֹל | **תַּקְטִיל** |
| 2fs | תִּקְטְלִי | **תַּקְטִילִי** |
| 2mp | תִּקְטְלוּ | **תַּקְטִילוּ** |
| 2fp | תִּקְטֹלְנָה | **תַּקְטֵלְנָה** |
| 1cs | אֶקְטֹל | **אַקְטִיל** |
| 1cp | נִקְטֹל | **נַקְטִיל** |

> **Key marker:** *Patach* under prefix consonant (יַ/תַּ/אַ/נַ) vs. Qal's *hiriq* (יִ/תִּ/אֶ/נִ). Final *chiriq* in Hiphil vs. *holem* in Qal.

## Wayyiqtol

*Sequential narrative form of the imperfect. Hiphil prefix: וַיַּ; final syllable shifts from chiriq to tsere.*

| Person | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | וַיִּקְטֹל | **וַיַּקְטֵל** |
| 3fs | וַתִּקְטֹל | **וַתַּקְטֵל** |
| 3mp | וַיִּקְטְלוּ | **וַיַּקְטִילוּ** |
| 3fp | וַתִּקְטֹלְנָה | **וַתַּקְטֵלְנָה** |
| 2ms | וַתִּקְטֹל | **וַתַּקְטֵל** |
| 2mp | וַתִּקְטְלוּ | **וַתַּקְטִילוּ** |
| 1cs | וָאֶקְטֹל | **וָאַקְטֵל** |
| 1cp | וַנִּקְטֹל | **וַנַּקְטֵל** |

> **Key marker:** Qal uses *hiriq* under prefix (וַיִּ); Hiphil uses *patach* (וַיַּ) + *tsere* in final syllable. The vowel shift chiriq → tsere from the Hiphil yiqtol is the diagnostic difference within the Hiphil itself.

## Weqatal

*Sequential future form of the perfect. וְ + perfect form; used for sequential or consequential future.*

| Person | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | וְקָטַל | **וְהִקְטִיל** |
| 3fs | וְקָטְלָה | **וְהִקְטִילָה** |
| 3cp | וְקָטְלוּ | **וְהִקְטִילוּ** |
| 2ms | וְקָטַלְתָּ | **וְהִקְטַלְתָּ** |
| 2mp | וּקְטַלְתֶּם | **וְהִקְטַלְתֶּם** |
| 1cs | וְקָטַלְתִּי | **וְהִקְטַלְתִּי** |
| 1cp | וְקָטַלְנוּ | **וְהִקְטַלְנוּ** |

> **Key marker:** Hiphil weqatal = וְ + perfect paradigm with הִ prefix. Context (future/sequential meaning) distinguishes it from a simple perfect with vav-conjunction.

## Imperative

*BBH §26.7 — Hiphil prefix: הַ; tsere in final syllable (2ms)*

| Form | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| 2ms | קְטֹל | **הַקְטֵל** |
| 2fs | קִטְלִי | **הַקְטִילִי** |
| 2mp | קִטְלוּ | **הַקְטִילוּ** |
| 2fp | קְטֹלְנָה | **הַקְטֵלְנָה** |

> **Key marker:** Qal imperative has no prefix (bare stem); Hiphil takes הַ prefix. The הַ prefix + *tsere* final (2ms) or *chiriq* (2fs/2mp) is the Hiphil signature.

## Infinitive Construct

*BBH §26.9 — Hiphil prefix: הַ; chiriq in final syllable*

| Form | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| Base | קְטֹל | **הַקְטִיל** |
| With לְ | לִקְטֹל | **לְהַקְטִיל** |
| With בְּ | בִּקְטֹל | **בְּהַקְטִיל** |
| With כְּ | כִּקְטֹל | **כְּהַקְטִיל** |

> **Key marker:** Hiphil inf. construct הַ prefix + *chiriq* final. Nearly identical in form to the Hiphil perfect 3ms (הִקְטִיל) — distinguished by the vowel under הַ (*patach* in inf. construct, *hiriq* in perfect).

---

## Infinitive Absolute

*BBH §26.11 — Hiphil prefix: הַ; tsere in final syllable*

| Form | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | קָטוֹל | **הַקְטֵל** |

> **Usage:** Appears before or after a finite verb of the same root for emphasis (הַרְבָּה אַרְבֶּה "I will surely multiply"). Qal inf. absolute uses *qamets-holem* (קָטוֹל); Hiphil uses הַ + *tsere* (הַקְטֵל), identical in form to the Hiphil imperative 2ms.

## Participle (Active)

*BBH §26.13 — Hiphil prefix: מַ; chiriq in final syllable*

| Form | Qal | **Hiphil** |
|---|---|---|
| ms | קֹטֵל | **מַקְטִיל** |
| fs | קֹטֶלֶת | **מַקְטֶלֶת** |
| mp | קֹטְלִים | **מַקְטִילִים** |
| fp | קֹטְלוֹת | **מַקְטִילוֹת** |

> **Key marker:** Qal participle has no prefix and uses *holem* (קֹ). Hiphil takes מַ prefix + *chiriq* (ms) or *tsere-seghol* (fs). Distinguish from Piel participle (מְקַטֵּל — *shewa* under מְ vs. *patach* under מַ).

## Summary: Qal vs. Hiphil at a Glance

| Conjugation | Qal marker | **Hiphil marker** |
|---|---|---|
| Perfect | קָ / *patach-patach* | **הִ + *chiriq*** |
| Imperfect | יִ / *hiriq* + *holem* | **יַ / *patach* + *chiriq*** |
| Wayyiqtol | וַיִּ + *holem* | **וַיַּ + *tsere*** |
| Weqatal | וְקָ | **וְהִ** |
| Imperative | no prefix + *holem* | **הַ + *tsere* (2ms)** |
| Inf. Construct | קְטֹל | **הַקְטִיל** |
| Inf. Absolute | קָטוֹל | **הַקְטֵל** |
| Participle ms | קֹטֵל / no prefix | **מַקְטִיל / מַ prefix + *chiriq*** |
