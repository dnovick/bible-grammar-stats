# Wayyiqtol and Weqatal Paradigms — BBH Chapter 17

---

## Wayyiqtol — Strong Verb (קטל)

The wayyiqtol is formed from the short imperfect (jussive base) with וַ + dagesh forte in the prefix consonant.

| Person | Imperfect (base) | Wayyiqtol | Prefix letter |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יִקְטֹל | וַיִּקְטֹל | יּ (dagesh forte) |
| 3fs | תִּקְטֹל | וַתִּקְטֹל | תּ |
| 2ms | תִּקְטֹל | וַתִּקְטֹל | תּ (= 3fs) |
| 2fs | תִּקְטְלִי | וַתִּקְטְלִי | תּ |
| 1cs | אֶקְטֹל | וָאֶקְטֹל | א (no dagesh; waw → **וָ**) |
| 3mp | יִקְטְלוּ | וַיִּקְטְלוּ | יּ |
| 3fp | תִּקְטֹלְנָה | וַתִּקְטֹלְנָה | תּ |
| 2mp | תִּקְטְלוּ | וַתִּקְטְלוּ | תּ |
| 2fp | תִּקְטֹלְנָה | וַתִּקְטֹלְנָה | תּ |
| 1cp | נִקְטֹל | וַנִּקְטֹל | נּ |

> **Key marker:** וַ + dagesh forte in the prefix consonant. The 1cs is the only exception: waw takes **qamets** (וָ) before aleph, which cannot take dagesh.

## Wayyiqtol — Weak Verb Forms

For roots that have a distinct short (jussive) Imperfect, the Wayyiqtol uses the **short form**.

### III-ה roots (e.g., בנה)

| Person | Long Imperfect | Short Jussive | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יִבְנֶה | יִבֶּן | וַיִּבֶן |
| 3fs | תִּבְנֶה | תִּבֶּן | וַתִּבֶן |
| 2ms | תִּבְנֶה | תִּבֶּן | וַתִּבֶן |
| 3mp | יִבְנוּ | — | וַיִּבְנוּ |
| 1cs | אֶבְנֶה | — | וָאֶבֶן |

> **Key marker:** The final ה is **absent** in the wayyiqtol. Compare: יִבְנֶה (Imperfect) vs. וַיִּבֶן (Wayyiqtol).

### Biconsonantal roots (e.g., קום)

| Person | Long Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יָקוּם | וַיָּ֫קׇם |
| 3fs | תָּקוּם | וַתָּ֫קׇם |
| 3mp | יָקוּמוּ | וַיָּקֻמוּ |
| 1cs | אָקוּם | וָאָ֫קׇם |

> **Key marker:** Wayyiqtol uses the **short form** — qamets hatuf (ׇ) under the root consonant instead of the long shureq (וּ) of the imperfect.

### Biconsonantal — בוא (most frequent)

| Person | Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יָבוֹא | וַיָּבֹא |
| 3fs | תָּבוֹא | וַתָּבֹא |
| 3mp | יָבֹאוּ | וַיָּבֹאוּ |
| 1cs | אָבוֹא | וָאָבֹא |

### I-יוד roots (e.g., הלך, ילד)

| Person | Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms (הלך) | יֵלֵךְ | וַיֵּלֶךְ |
| 3ms (ילד) | יֵלֵד | וַיֵּלֶד |
| 3mp (הלך) | יֵלְכוּ | וַיֵּלְכוּ |

> **Key marker:** Tsere under waw-consecutive prefix consonant: וַיֵּ– (not וַיִּ–).

### I-נ roots (e.g., נתן)

| Person | Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יִתֵּן | וַיִּתֵּן |
| 3fs | תִּתֵּן | וַתִּתֵּן |
| 3mp | יִתְּנוּ | וַיִּתְּנוּ |

> **Key marker:** Dagesh forte in R2 from nun assimilation — same in both Imperfect and Wayyiqtol.

### I-aleph roots (e.g., אמר — the most frequent wayyiqtol)

| Person | Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יֹאמַר | וַיֹּאמֶר |
| 3fs | תֹּאמַר | וַתֹּאמֶר |
| 3mp | יֹאמְרוּ | וַיֹּאמְרוּ |
| 1cs | אֹמַר | וָאֹמַר |

> **Key marker:** Holem under prefix (not hireq); seghol under mem in wayyiqtol 3ms: וַיֹּאמֶר.

### Geminate roots (e.g., סבב)

| Person | Imperfect | Wayyiqtol |
|---|---|---|
| 3ms | יִסֹּב / יָסֹב | וַיָּ֫סׇּב |
| 3mp | יִסֹּבּוּ | וַיִּסֹּבּוּ |

## Weqatal — Strong Verb (שמר)

The weqatal is morphologically identical to the perfect + conjunction וְ. Stress shifts to the ultima (final syllable) in multi-syllable forms.

| Person | Perfect | Weqatal | Stress |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3ms | שָׁמַר | וְשָׁמַר | no shift (monosyllabic stem) |
| 3fs | שָׁמְרָה | וְשָׁמְרָה | — |
| 2ms | שָׁמַ֫רְתָּ | וְשָׁמַרְתָּ֫ | shifts to tā |
| 2fs | שָׁמַ֫רְתְּ | וְשָׁמַרְתְּ֫ | |
| 1cs | שָׁמַ֫רְתִּי | וְשָׁמַרְתִּ֫י | shifts to tî |
| 3cp | שָׁמְרוּ | וְשָׁמְרוּ | — |
| 2mp | שְׁמַרְתֶּ֫ם | וּשְׁמַרְתֶּ֫ם | waw → וּ before shewa |
| 2fp | שְׁמַרְתֶּ֫ן | וּשְׁמַרְתֶּ֫ן | waw → וּ before shewa |
| 1cp | שָׁמַ֫רְנוּ | וְשָׁמַרְנוּ֫ | shifts to nû |

> **Key marker:** Context — weqatal follows an imperfect, imperative, or jussive and carries future/consequential sense. Stress on the final syllable (distinguished from perfect's penultimate stress in oral reading).

## Summary

| Form | Prefix | Dagesh in prefix? | Vowel under prefix | Stress |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Imperfect | יִ/תִּ/אֶ/נִ | — | hireq (usually) | penult |
| Wayyiqtol | וַ + | **Yes** (except aleph/resh) | like jussive/short Impf. | penult |
| Perfect | — | — | qamets (R1) | penult |
| Weqatal | וְ/וּ + | — | like perfect | **ultima** |

### Quick ID mnemonic

- **See וַ + dagesh?** → Wayyiqtol (sequential past)
- **See וְ + perfect pattern, future context?** → Weqatal (sequential future)
- **No waw prefix, imperfect pattern?** → Simple Imperfect
- **No waw prefix, perfect pattern, past context?** → Perfect (qatal)
