## Group 8 — Disambiguation and Functional Patterns

| # | Hebrew | Answer |
|---|---|---|
| 26 | שָׁמ֣וֹר (in isolation) | Qal IA of שמר — qamets under R1 distinguishes it from IC/Imper. שְׁמֹר (shewa) and Perfect שָׁמַ֣ר (qamets + patach) |
| 27 | שָׁמַ֣ר vs. שָׁמ֣וֹר | Perfect 3ms vs. IA: שָׁמַ֣ר = qamets + patach; שָׁמ֣וֹר = qamets + holem-waw — the R2 vowel distinguishes them |
| 28 | זָכ֕וֹר אֶת-יוֹם הַשַּׁבָּת | Imperatival IA (Exo 20:8) — IA used as command (no paired finite verb of same root); contrast שָׁמ֣וֹר תִּשְׁמְר֗וּן (emphatic: IA + finite verb) |
| 29 | שָׁמ֗וֹר vs. שָׁמ֣וֹר תִּשְׁמְר֗וּן | Imperatival IA vs. Emphatic IA: stand-alone IA = command; IA + finite verb of same root = emphatic intensifier |
| 30 | מ֥וֹת preceded by לָ | IC, not IA — the preposition לָ forces IC reading; IA never takes a preposition prefix |
| 31 | רָאֹ֣ה רָאִ֛יתִי | Emphatic IA construction · ראה · III-ה · "I have surely seen" (Exo 3:7) — IA + Perfect 1cs; burning bush |
| 32 | טָרֹ֥ף טֹרַ֖ף | Emphatic IA construction · טרף · Strong A · "surely been torn" (Gen 44:28) — IA + Passive Perfect; dramatic narrative |
